X10'15 program posted

The program for the X10'15 workshop is now available.

The workshop will be held on Sunday 14 June in Portland, OR (co-located with PLDI'15).

You can now register for the workshop and associated events via the FCRC registration site. The early registration deadline (lower registration fees) is May 18.
